Perfumes,the best perfumes ever?

In everyday language, a perfume is a type of fragrance that people wear to smell nice. Perfumes usually come in small bottles and have a mix of different scents, like flowers, fruits, spices,or woods. They can be light and fresh or strong and sweet, and they’re used to express personality, mood, or style without saying a word.
