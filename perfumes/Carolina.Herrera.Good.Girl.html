Good Girl

Good Girl Blush Blue is a fresh and sparkling fragrance that combines bright citrus and delicate floral notes, creating a lively and modern scent. Its blend of jasmine and tuberose adds a soft, feminine touch, while the warm vanilla base gives it a comforting finish. Packaged in the signature stiletto-shaped bottle with a cool blue hue, it’s perfect for confident women who love a balance of freshness and elegance.
