Devine

Jean Paul Gaultier Divine is a bold and radiant fragrance that celebrates powerful femininity. With notes of white flowers, salty undertones, and sweet meringue, it strikes a perfect balance between elegance and daring. Housed in the iconic corset-shaped bottle, it’s a scent that feels both modern and timeless, designed for women who embrace their strength and beauty.
