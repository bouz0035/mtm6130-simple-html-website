Black opium

Black Opium is a bold and addictive fragrance that blends rich coffee, sweet vanilla, and soft white florals to create a scent that’s both edgy and sensual. It’s perfect for those who love a deep, warm perfume with a modern, rebellious twist. Mysterious and captivating, it leaves a lasting impression wherever you go.
